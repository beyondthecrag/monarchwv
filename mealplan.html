Chili

Ingredients

-1 tsp oil
-1/2 onion
-2 small potatoes
-1 can beans
-2 cans diced tomato
-1 cup dry red lentils
-2 cups water
-1 tbsp chili powder
-1 tsp paprika
-2 tsp bouilon cubes

Instructions

-Heat oil in a large pot on medium-high heat, add onion. Cook for about 2-3 minutes.
-Add potatoes, beans, diced tomato, lentils, and water. Bring to boil.
-Once boiling, turn down to simmer and add seasonings.
-Cover and simmer for 30-40 minutes stirring occasionally.

Soup

Ingredients

-1/4 cup olive oil (or enough to cover the bottom of the pot)
-1 onion
-8 cups of water
-3 vegan bouilon cubes
-1/2 tsp black pepper
-1/2 tsp salt
-8 ounces of noodles
-4 medium carrots
-4 stalks of celery
-2 1/2 cups of vegan chicken
-(or) 1 can of chickpeas

Instructions

-In a large pot, heat oil on medium heat. Slice carrots, celery, and onion. Add to pot. Saute about 5 minutes.
-Turn heat to medium-high until simmering, add water, bouilon, pepper, salt. Add chicken (or chickpeas) and noodles, cook until noodles are done according to the box.

Bean Burger

Ingredients

-1 can of beans
-1 cup of pasta sauce
-1 cup of oat flour (blend oats to make)
-1 tsp oil

Instructions

Mash up beans by hand or with a food processor.
-In a mixing bowl, add mashed beans, oat flour, and pasta sauce. Add in garlic powder or seasonings as desired.
-Form mixture into patties by hand.
-Heat oil in pan on medium heat
-Add patties and flatten into burger shape with a spatula
-Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side

Overnight Oats

Ingredients

-1/2 cup oats
-1 tbsp chia/hemp/flax seeds
-3/4 cup non-dair milk
-1/2 tsp vanilla extract
-1 tbsp maple syrup (optional)
-1 tbsp of your favorite jam

Instructions

-In a jar or lidded container, add oats and seeds.
-Pour in milk, vanilla, and syrup
-Add in your jam of choice.
-Put in fridge for 3-4 hours, up to 72 hours.

Tofu Scramble

Ingredients

-1 tbsp olive oil
-16 oz firm/extra-firm tofu
-2 tbsp nutritional yeast
-1/4 tsp tumeric
-1/2 tsp salt
-1/4 tsp garlic powder
-2 tbsp non-dairy milk

Instructions

-Heat olive oil on medium heat.
-Mash block of tofu in the pan.
-Add nutritional yeast, tumeric, salt, and garlic powder.
-Cook and stir for about 5 minutes.
-Pour non-dairy milk into pan and stir to mix.

Chickpea Pasta

Ingredients

-1 box pasta of your choice
-1 cup red sauce
-1 cup chickpeas
-handful of spinach

Instructions

-Boil pasta according to box.
-Drain and rinse chickpeas, rinse spinach.
-Drain pasta and in the same pot add pasta sauce, chickpeas, and spinach.
