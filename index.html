Welcome to the official Monarch website! This organization was started by a group of students attending West Virginia University. Due to the low percentage of vegans we believe students should have a safe environment to connect with other vegans!

This website stands as a one-stop spot for any and all information related to the org as well as resources for vegans and the veg-curious.

On the side of the page you will find our social media links! We strongly encourage all members to participate in our Discord server. Discord is free of external activity which helps us stay clear of emotionally triggering social media activity!
